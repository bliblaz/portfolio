# Hello World 👋

This is my first blog post. I've been meaning to write about the things I build and learn for a while now — so here we are.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about:

- **Full-stack development** — patterns, pitfalls, and real-world lessons
- **DevOps & infrastructure** — Kubernetes, Docker, CI/CD from the trenches
- **Side projects** — what I'm building and why
- **Things I find interesting** — from AI to robotics to random deep dives

## Why a blog?

Writing forces you to think clearly. If I can't explain something in a blog post, I probably don't understand it well enough.

Plus, I've benefited so much from other developers sharing their knowledge — time to give back.

Stay tuned. ✌️
